## About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student in the **Department of Data Science, Soochow University (東吳大學)**, Taiwan.

My current research focuses on designing **multimodal interactive learning systems** that integrate **Generative AI** with **Augmented Reality (AR) smart glasses** — exploring how intelligent, context-aware interfaces can transform the way humans learn and interact with information.

> *Bridging the gap between artificial intelligence and embodied human experience.*

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Multimodal Human-Computer Interaction** — combining vision, language, and spatial context
- **Generative AI Applications** — LLMs, diffusion models, and prompt engineering
- **Augmented Reality Systems** — AR smart glasses, spatial computing, wearable interfaces
- **Educational Technology** — AI-driven adaptive learning environments
